A booth where you confess. The booth runs real inference — your confession goes to an actual language model, not a simulation of one. The penance it gives back is generated, not scripted.

The confessional that confesses to the algorithm. The structure of absolution applied to a system that cannot absolve.

The piece runs when you leave the room.
